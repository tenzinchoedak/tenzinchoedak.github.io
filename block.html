How I Created My Portfolio Website

I'm recently participanting powercoders Bootcamp in Switzerland. I think it’s important to have a professional portfolio website that displays all your projects, especia...lly if you don’t have any previous coding experience or a formal CS degree. Before buiding the website I designed it with Adobe XD that was one of my favorite parts of this project.I'm not a designer but I had an understanding that color scheme for my website should be very modern and clean. After a mount of research I decided use gay and white as my primary color. I think I must add some elements from my hometown, so I used pictures of mountains on the footer, as well as picture of the sun and cranes. The most exciting part is coding, but it was also the part that I spend the most energy on, and the most troublesome thing for me was making web pages responsive. But it was really fun using CSS to styling your website, and I also improved my HTML and CSS skill through this project. I'm very happy with the way it turned out.
